## [[Blog Post -- Section 15 - Gas Town Workflow]]


What is the most fundamental workflow in Gas Town?
?
The [[Term -- handoff|handoff]] — `gt handoff`, `/handoff`, or just say "let's hand off".
<!--GUID:94d296bce4c29-->

<!--MEADOW_SR_GUID:9ee657ebc306c-->


What happens when a worker performs a [[Term -- handoff|handoff]]?
?
It optionally sends itself work, then restarts its [[Term -- Session|Session]] right there in tmux.
<!--GUID:f0c50339cd0cf-->

<!--MEADOW_SR_GUID:1d51a11338969-->


Which workers require you to explicitly tell them it's time to [[Term -- handoff|hand off]]?
?
The [[Term -- The Mayor|Mayor]], your [[Term -- The Crew|Crew]], and sometimes others you direct.
<!--GUID:d44beac89c334-->
