## [[Blog Post -- Section 7 - The GUPP Nudge]]

What is the solution for Claude code not running an agent's [[Term -- Hook|Hook]], and instead waiting for user input?
?
[[Term -- GUPP Nudge|GUPP Nudge]]
<!--GUID:e906fe2a0eed8-->

<!--MEADOW_SR_GUID:9af5bc7e6c1f0-->


When an [[Term -- Agent|agent]] boots up, what are the two things it must read?
?
Its [[Term -- Inbox|Inbox]] and [[Term -- Hook|Hook]].
<!--GUID:f17738a039bf4-->

<!--MEADOW_SR_GUID:3dae274e83e54-->


What principle are workers prompted to follow regarding startup behavior?
?
[[gt/principle - physics over politeness|Physics over politeness]] — if their [[Term -- Hook|hook]] has work, they must start working without waiting.
<!--GUID:83717a607f835-->

<!--MEADOW_SR_GUID:49410684e6ded-->


What does `gt nudge` do technically?
?
Sends a tmux notification to a worker (or a whole channel) as if the user had typed it.
<!--GUID:dab193635fdbf-->

<!--MEADOW_SR_GUID:641ccec8eb617-->


What two mechanisms together keep [[Term -- Gastown Universal Propulsion Principle - GUPP|GUPP]] running reliably?
?
The [[Term -- GUPP Nudge|GUPP Nudge]] and the hierarchical heartbeat from [[Term -- The Deacon|the Deacon]] downward.
<!--GUID:ee04f86026d55-->

<!--MEADOW_SR_GUID:d86e9f98c141a-->


What enables [[Term -- Worker|workers]] to continue [[Term -- Molecule|molecules]] across [[Term -- Session|sessions]]?
?
[[Term -- Gastown Universal Propulsion Principle - GUPP|GUPP]] — as long as work is on their [[Term -- Hook|hook]], any new session picks it up and continues.
<!--GUID:71638b36fd287-->
