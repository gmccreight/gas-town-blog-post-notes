^ [[Term]] -- Agent Bead

---

**Agent Bead**: A worker's persistent identity in [[project - Beads]] (i.e. in Git). Each Gas Town worker has its own Agent Bead. Along with [[Term -- Role Bead|Role Bead]]s and [[Term -- Hook|Hook]]s, Agent Beads are examples of [[Term -- Pinned Bead|Pinned Bead]]s — they float like sticky notes in the Beads data plane and never get closed unless the identity goes away.
