**Polecats**: Gas Town is a [[work-swarming engine]]. Polecats are ephemeral per-[[Term -- Rigs|Rig]] workers that spin up on demand. Polecats work, often in swarms, to produce [[Term -- Merge Request - MR|Merge Request - MR]]s, then hand them off to the [[Term -- Merge Queue - MQ|Merge Queue - MQ]]. After the merge they are fully decommissioned, though their names are recycled
