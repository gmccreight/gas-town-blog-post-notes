^ [[Term]] -- Town Mail

---

[[project - Beads|Bead]]-backed messaging between Gas Town agents via inboxes. Town mail and [[Term -- Messaging|messaging]] (events) use [[project - Beads|Beads]], as do other types of orchestration. As the [[Term -- The Overseer|Overseer]], you have an identity in the system, and your own inbox, and you can send and receive town mail.
