## [[Blog Post -- Section 14 - Convoys]]


What is the primary difference between a [[Term -- Convoy|Convoy]] and an [[Term -- Epic|Epic]]?
?
The tracked issues in a [[Term -- Convoy|Convoy]] are not its children — most already have another parent.
<!--GUID:a2adb0b3d6fa6-->

<!--MEADOW_SR_GUID:798a06b474a4e-->


What problem did [[Term -- Convoy|Convoy]]s solve for the [[activity feed]]?
?
It's confusing to hear "issue `wy-a7je4` just finished" — wrapping every unit of [[Term -- slinging|slung]] work with a [[Term -- Convoy|Convoy]] gives a meaningful delivery unit to track.
<!--GUID:271f76038a11e-->

<!--MEADOW_SR_GUID:2f66bf23bd92f-->


In a traditional [[software development lifecycle - SDLC]], what does a [[Term -- Convoy|Convoy]] most closely align to?
?
A feature.
<!--GUID:07d13ef4c72dd-->

<!--MEADOW_SR_GUID:589a57b27b421-->


What is a [[Term -- Swarm|Swarm]]?
?
Ephemeral [[Term -- Agent|Agent]] [[Term -- Session|Session]]s taking on persistent work.
<!--GUID:3a35b77456e97-->

<!--MEADOW_SR_GUID:1adb6ac0d4516-->


What command is the fundamental primitive for moving work around in Gas Town?
?
[[Term -- slinging|sling]] — it files work and sends it to a worker immediately.
<!--GUID:215c8417bc620-->

<!--MEADOW_SR_GUID:ba21d5f888575-->


What happens to a [[Term -- Polecats|Polecat]] when its [[Term -- Molecule|Molecule]] enters a [[Term -- Gate state|Gate state]]?
?
The [[Term -- Polecats|Polecat]] disappears. When the [[Term -- Gate bead|Gate bead]] triggers, Gas Town wakes up a new [[Term -- Polecats|Polecat]] to continue the work.
<!--GUID:dced33528594a-->

<!--MEADOW_SR_GUID:caca8885c9754-->


Can multiple [[Term -- Swarm|Swarm]]s work on the same [[Term -- Convoy|Convoy]]?
?
Yes — whoever manages the [[Term -- Convoy|Convoy]] (e.g. [[Term -- The Witness|Witness]]) keeps recycling [[Term -- Polecats|polecats]] and pushing them on issues until it finishes.
<!--GUID:c2dc9f3296b3c-->
