^ [[Term]] -- Seance

---

When a [[Term -- handoff|handoff]] or [[Term -- self-handoff|self-handoff]] goes wrong, then a Seance may be required.

It spins up Claude Code in a subprocess, uses `/resume` to revive the predecessor session, then asks it where the handoff materials are.

This is a solution for the problem: [[agent problem - sometimes agents don't tie up their work properly at the end]]
