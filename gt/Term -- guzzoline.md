^ [[Term]] -- guzzoline

---

**Guzzoline:** A word to represent “molecularized work” — work in the form that [[Term -- Agent|Agent]]s can pick and complete a step at a time. It’s work that you can compose together, [[Term -- Molecule|Molecule]]s bonding with other molecules, and you can set up the dependencies for an entire gigantic project in advance, and have Gas Town swarm it for an entire weekend, unattended, if you’re brave enough.
