^ [[Term]] -- The Deacon

---

**The Deacon** is a Patrol Agent: it runs a [[Term -- Patrol|Patrol]] (a well-defined workflow) in a loop. Gas Town has a daemon that pings the Deacon every couple minutes and says, “Do your job.” The Deacon intelligently propagates this DYFJ signal downward to the other town workers, ensuring Gas Town stays working.
