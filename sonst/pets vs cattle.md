Pets vs Cattle

---

A metaphor from the Kubernetes / cloud-native world. **Pets** are long-lived, individually named, and carefully maintained. **Cattle** are ephemeral, interchangeable, and disposable — if one dies you spin up another.

In Gas Town, [[Term -- Agent|agents]] are pets (persistent identities in [[project - Beads]]) while [[Term -- Session|sessions]] are cattle (ephemeral Claude Code sessions thrown at persistent work).
